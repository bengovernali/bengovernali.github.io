About Me

All my life I have been a builder. From my early days assembling lego sets, to fabricating prototypes in my engineering classes, I have never stopped creating.

After some time working in project mangement roles in the aerospace industry, a friend recommended I look into front end development. After a few months of teaching myself the fundamentals I knew this was going to be my path in life.

I attended the Digitalcrafts Coding Bootcamp and furthered my education by expanding into the back end. With my newly aquired skills, as well as my past experience to guide me, I am excited to get out there and keep building!
